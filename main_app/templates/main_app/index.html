Welcome to Kurdistan Dog Rescue!

At Kurdistan Dog Rescue, we are dedicated to rescuing and rehoming dogs in need. Our organization is committed to providing a safe and loving environment for these wonderful animals, while also connecting them with caring and responsible forever homes. We believe that every dog deserves a second chance at a happy and fulfilling life.

Adopt a Furry Friend!

If you're looking to add a furry friend to your family, we have a wide range of lovable dogs available for adoption. Our dogs come from various backgrounds and circumstances, but they all share one thing in common - they're ready to find their forever homes. Browse through our adoption gallery to meet our adorable dogs and learn more about their personalities and stories. When you adopt from us, you not only gain a loyal companion but also make a significant difference in the life of a rescued dog.

Support Our Cause:

As a non-profit organization, we rely on the generosity of animal lovers like you to continue our life-saving work. Your support enables us to provide medical care, shelter, food, and love to the dogs in our care. Whether you choose to make a donation, volunteer your time, or spread awareness about our cause, every contribution helps us make a difference in the lives of these deserving animals. Together, we can create a brighter future for dogs in need.

Join Our Community:

We invite you to be part of our passionate community of dog lovers. Follow us on social media to stay updated on our latest rescues, adoption events, and heartwarming stories. Share our posts, engage with our content, and help us spread the word about the importance of rescue and adoption. By joining our community, you become an advocate for these incredible dogs and inspire others to make a difference.

Thank you for visiting Kurdistan Dog Rescue. Together, we can give these deserving dogs the love and care they deserve, one adoption at a time.
